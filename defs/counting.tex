\Definition{Binomial coefficient}\label{aff0ae6}

Denoted by $\binom nk$ an read as ``$n$ choose $k$", we define
$$
  \binom nk:=\frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$

This calculates how many ways there are to choose $k$ items from $n$ unique
items.
